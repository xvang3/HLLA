About Us

The Hmong language faces a decline as younger generations increasingly assimilate into other cultures, leading to a loss of linguistic and cultural identity. Hmong Roots aims to combat this by providing an engaging and interactive platform to teach and preserve the Hmong Language.


Hmong Roots is a web application and language learning resource geared towards teaching young children about the Hmong Culture and Language.


Our curriculum currently covers the following categories:

  • 8 Tone Markers
  • 6 Single Vowels
  • 7 Double Vowels
  • 17 Single Consonants
  • 4 Common Phrases
  • Numbers (0-10)
  • 14 Family Members (Male and Female uses)
  • 5 Animals

(Double, Triple and Quadruple Consonants TBA)
